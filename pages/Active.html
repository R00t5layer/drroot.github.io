Let’s start with impacket and Kerberos attack → Kerberoasting

Abstract: With kerberoasting attacks we are able to capture the TGS, that TGS may containg User and passwords

  • KDC (Key Distribution Center)**: This is the central server that handles authentication in Kerberos. Think of it as the “gatekeeper” that verifies identities and issues tickets to access services.
  • TGT (Ticket Granting Ticket)**: This is a special ticket that you get after authenticating with the KDC. This ticket allows you to request tickets for specific services without having to re-authenticate.
  • TGS (Ticket Granting Service)**: This is the service that gives you tickets to access specific services (such as a file server or email) once you have submitted your TGT. Within this TGS you can find username and passwords.
